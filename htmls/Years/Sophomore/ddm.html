Teacher Voting App

This app is a classroom voting app, which means there are two versions of the app. The first version is a version made for the teacher to put a question out for students to vote on. The second version is for the students to vote yes or no on the proposed question by the teacher. By clicking a thumb up or thumb down, on the bottom it displays the vote count.
